Whitehaven Beach, Australia

Whitehaven Beach is a 7 km stretch along Whitsunday Island, Australia.
The island is accessible by boat, seaplane & helicopter from Airlie Beach, as well as Hamilton Island.
It lies across from Stockyard Beach, better known as Chalkie's Beach, on Haslewood Island.
The beach is known for its crystal white silica sands and turquoise coloured waters.
The beach has barbeque and camping facilities.
